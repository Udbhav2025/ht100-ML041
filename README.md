Title: The Cardio Predictor: Heart Health Early Warning
ID: udbhav_015

Mission:
Heart attacks often strike without warning. This tool helps doctors identify at-risk patients years in advance by analyzing medical history to calculate personalized risk scores.

MVP Requirements:
• Risk Scoring: Output probability score for heart risk
• Data Handling: Work accurately even with missing patient data
• Doctor Dashboard: Present risk factors clearly

Tech Stack:
• Random Forest
• Logistic Regression
• UCI Heart Disease Dataset
